THE ARTIST
Ashikur is a poet, visual artist, and editor from Assam, India. His sketches move between horror manga darkness and soft, intimate warmth — sometimes in the same piece.
THE MEDIUM
Digital illustration with ink-like marks. Each work sits at the edge of language — what the poems couldn't say, the drawings hold.
THE CONTEXT
Editor-in-Chief of as flowers literary magazine. Rooted in Assamese identity, queer literature, and a horror manga aesthetic that refuses to be just one thing.
